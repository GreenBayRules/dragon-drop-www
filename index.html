Welcome To DragonDrop

DragonDrop is a online tool you can use to create your own professional websites in minutes without needing any coding knowledge. Just drag and drop some simple elements onto the screen and there you'll have it, your own website ready for the whole world to see. We love seeing your creations, so get creative!

No programming or development knowledge needed, our mascot, Drop, will show you how to do things even if you don't know anything in the website industry. |




Features
  • Easy to use drag'n'drop website creator - You can now easily create your very own websites with this tool, it takes no coding knowledge whatsoever and you can have it up on the internet in minutes. We encourage you to be creative and our website studio can be used to create anything from a blog to a showcase.
  • Features for Scratch Users - We have made it easier than ever for you to create your own personal website for your Scratch account. By connecting your Scratch account, you get special elements to make your website have special fetures connected to your Scratch account.
  • Always have Drop the Dragon helping you learn how to make your website the best.
  • Support - We will support you from when you first start creating your website until the time you finish with your website, if you have an enquiry about DragonDrop or have found a bug our team will be ready to listen to you whatever time of the day!
